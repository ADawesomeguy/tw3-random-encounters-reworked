Hordes contracts

Hordes contracts, like the nest contracts, are simple. Once picked from a board the game will wait for you to leave the settlement to look for the nearby creatures that are causing troubles to the villagers. After some time you will see a bar showing you the progress of the hordes and monsters will quickly swarm you.

Your goal is to eradicate the horde and survive. If you die the contract is cancelled and you will have to pick the notice again.
