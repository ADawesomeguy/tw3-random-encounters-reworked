Nests contracts

RER Nests contracts are simple, when you pick the nest notice on the board you will get a new marker on your minimap that will point to an area you should search. In that area you will find a nest that spawns monsters, your goal is to destroy the nest.

The nest will continue to spawn more creatures as long as it is alive. However after a certain time creatures will stop giving loot, and finally the nest will stop spawning anything.

During nest contracts you will only see small creatures.
